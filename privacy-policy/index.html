Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This privacy policy explains how missingfootball.com ("we", "us", "our") collects and uses information when you visit our website.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information. Your game progress, streaks, and statistics are stored locally on your device using your browser's localStorage and are never transmitted to us.

Cookies and Analytics

We use Google Analytics and Google AdSense, which may set cookies on your device to help us understand how visitors use our site and to display relevant advertisements. These services may collect anonymised data such as your IP address, browser type, and pages visited. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Advertising

We may display advertisements on this site served by Google AdSense and/or third-party affiliate partners. These advertisers may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings.

Responsible Gambling

Some advertisements on this site may relate to sports betting. All betting-related content is intended for adults aged 18 and over only. If you or someone you know has a gambling problem, please visit BeGambleAware.org or call the National Gambling Helpline on 0808 8020 133.

Third-Party Links

Our site may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites.

Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
